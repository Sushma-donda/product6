 What Makes a Waffle Belgian
                    What Is a Belgian Waffle? Belgian waffles are beloved for their extra-deep pockets—the better for filling with
                     butter, jam, or maple syrup. They are often thicker than their American counterparts, and made with a yeasted 
                     batter and crunchy pearl sugar. There's nothing quite like a classic Belgian waffle.
